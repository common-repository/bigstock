== Description ==

A Wordpress Plugin for [Bigstock](http://www.bigstockphoto.com/)

Easily search our collection of over 31 million royalty-free & insert images and
insert into your pages and posts without ever leaving Wordpress.

This plugin uses your existing Bigstock subscription to license and download
images, so if you've already purchased an image prior to installing the plugin
you won't be required to purchase the same image again!

Simply navigate to any post or page in the admin and click the “Bigstock Images”
button at the top of the editor. Sign in using your regular Bigstock credentials
and enjoy licensing royalty-free photography, vectors, and illustrations without
ever leaving Wordpress.

### Features:

* Over 31,000,000 royalty-free stock photos, illustrations, and vectors
* Insert directly into your posts or pages without leaving Wordpress
* Use your existing Bigstock subscription
* Insert watermarked preview images into your post before publishing
* Image attribution automatically added

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/bigstockphoto` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Navigate to any post or page in the admin and click the "Bigstock Images" button

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need to create a new account to use the Bigstock plugin on WordPress? =

Not at all! Any existing or new user account can log into the plugin and begin
downloading right away! If you do not have a Bigstock account, and would like to
sign up and check out the plugin, please visit www.bigstock.com to create an account!

= Can I use already existing subscriptions to license and download Bigstock images? =

Yes! Any existing or new Bigstock image subscription can be used with this plugin to license and download
images from our extensive collection.

= Can I redownload images that I have already licensed from Bigstock.com through the plugin? =

Yes again! Any and all of your current licenses can be used to redownload images through the plugin.

= Can I view and redownload images that I have licensed through the Bigstock WordPress plugin on the Bigstock website? =

Of course! Any images licensed and downloaded through the plugin can be viewed and redownloaded from Bigstock.com
free of charge!

= Can I use Bigstock Credits to license and download images through the plugin? =

Unfortunately, the plugin only accepts tokens from Bigstock image subscriptions. If you have credits
and are interested in a Bigstock subscription to use with this plugin, please visit www.bigstockphoto.com/subscribe
to find a low-cost subscription that best meets your needs.

= How can I give feedback or ask questions while I am using the plugin? =

Easily! Towards the bottom-right hand side of the plugin window, you should see a blue circle. Click
that to open a chat connection with a Bigstock representative who will be happy to answer your questions
or take your feedback! We love hearing feedback from our users and are excited to hear what you
think about our products!
